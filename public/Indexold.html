Welcome to FishMarket Connect

Linking Fishers to the Market.

We enhance the value chain of small-scale fisheries in Mombasa and connect them to the international market. Our main focus is on strengthening market linkages.

Objectives

1. Provide Real-Time Market Information: Ensure fishers have access to current market prices, demand forecasts, and trends to make informed decisions.

2. Enhance Market Access: Facilitate better market access by connecting fishers with international buyers and markets.

3. Improve Decision-Making: Empower fishers to plan their catch and sales strategies based on reliable market data.

4. Increase Income: By having access to real-time market information, fishers can optimize their sales for higher profits.
